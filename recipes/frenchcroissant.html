French Crossiant

Introduction to making French Croissant

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Water and Milk
  • Sugar and Honey

Instructions

  1. First, activate the yeast by dissolving it in the warm liquid and inverted sugar mixture, and let it “come alive”.
  2. Next, add the sugar, and melted and cooled butter.
  3. Followed by the flour, and finally the salt
  4. Mix the dough to from a scraggly, rough looking dough – this lets the flour hydrate
